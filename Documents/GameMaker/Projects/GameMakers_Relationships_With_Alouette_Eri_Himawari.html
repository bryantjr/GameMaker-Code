Game Maker’s Relationships

Game Maker’s connection with Alouette, Eri, and Himawari is not based on combat alone. These bonds represent family, trust, protection, and emotional support. They are his light during dark conflicts and reminders of why he fights.

Alouette (Mega Man Zero)

Age: 6 | Role: Supporter & Emotional Anchor

Alouette sees Game Maker as a safe and kind protector. Game Maker treats Alouette like a younger sister or daughter, always prioritizing her safety above all else.

Relationship Bond: Gentle Guardian & Trusted Family

He encourages her innocence, creativity, and kindness, shielding her from danger while allowing her to grow at her own pace.

Eri (My Hero Academia)

Age: 6 | Role: Healing Presence & Hope

Game Maker understands Eri’s painful past and vows never to let her be hurt again. He treats her with patience, warmth, and reassurance.

Relationship Bond: Protector & Emotional Healer

Eri feels safe around Game Maker, knowing he will always stand between her and danger. Her smile gives him strength during the hardest battles.

Himawari Uzumaki (Boruto: Next Generations)

Age: 10 | Role: Bright Spirit & Moral Support

Himawari views Game Maker as a dependable big brother figure. She brings warmth, optimism, and emotional balance to his life.

Relationship Bond: Protective Mentor & Family Ally

Game Maker encourages her strength, kindness, and confidence, while making sure she never faces danger alone.

Together, Alouette, Eri, and Himawari are not fighters on the battlefield— they are the heart of Game Maker’s world. Their presence reminds him that protecting life matters more than winning any battle.
