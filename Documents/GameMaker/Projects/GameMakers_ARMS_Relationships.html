Game Maker – ARMS Universe Relationships

Game Maker’s Role in ARMS:

Within the ARMS universe, Game Maker is known as a cross-world combat supporter and balance guardian. He respects the ARMS League as a place of fair competition and never interferes with matches, only assisting fighters outside the arena during large-scale threats.

Primary Bond – Min Min

Min Min holds a special place among Game Maker’s allies. He respects her discipline, focus, and determination both as a fighter and as a business owner. Game Maker often supports her training routines, helps defend her ramen shop from outside threats, and encourages her to balance strength with everyday life.

Their relationship is built on mutual respect, calm communication, and teamwork. Min Min views Game Maker as a reliable protector of peace, while Game Maker admires her independence and resolve.

Team Dynamics

Game Maker does not lead the ARMS League, but fighters acknowledge him as an outside protector who ensures no external forces disrupt their world. He assists with evacuation, defense, and crisis management when threats exceed tournament boundaries.

Relationship Summary

Game Maker’s strongest ARMS connection is with Min Min, symbolizing discipline, independence, and balance. Across the ARMS cast, he is trusted as a guardian of fairness, a supporter of growth, and a defender of peaceful competition.
