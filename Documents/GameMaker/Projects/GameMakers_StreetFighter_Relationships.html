Game Maker – Street Fighter Heroes Alliance

Alliance Status: Trusted Heroes

Game Maker shares strong alliances with heroic Street Fighter characters across all generations of the series, respecting their fighting spirit, courage, and honor. Only good-aligned characters are included.

Classic Street Fighter Heroes

  • Ryu – Symbol of discipline and mastery; mentor-like relationship with Game Maker
  • Ken – Loyal and cheerful fighter; Game Maker considers him a strong ally in combat
  • Chun-Li – Justice-driven, protects civilians; trusted strategist and friend to Game Maker
  • Guile – Military tactician; Game Maker values his dedication to peace
  • Blanka – Nature-protecting fighter; friendly and energetic ally to Game Maker
  • E. Honda – Sumo champion and honorable fighter; respected by Game Maker
  • Dhalsim – Spiritual guide; Game Maker respects his wisdom and calm nature
  • Sagat – Once a rival, now an ally; Game Maker trusts his strength and discipline
  • Ruby Heart – Mystical pirate with heroic tendencies; Game Maker values her courage and loyalty

Street Fighter Alpha Series Heroes

  • Cammy – Brave and disciplined agent; trusted friend and combat ally
  • Charlie Nash – Tactical and loyal; Game Maker respects his resilience
  • Rose – Mystic fighter with soul powers; ally in multiverse crises
  • Guy – Bushin martial artist; Game Maker admires his dedication to training and honor
  • Adon – Apprentice fighter; Game Maker encourages his growth toward heroism

Street Fighter III Heroes

  • Alex – Courageous wrestler; Game Maker values his tenacity and loyalty
  • Ibuki – Agile ninja; trusted young ally and supporter
  • Sean – Up-and-coming martial artist; Game Maker supports his training and growth
  • Makoto – Fierce and honorable; strong ally in combat scenarios

Street Fighter IV & V Heroes

  • Elena – Peaceful healer and fighter; Game Maker admires her harmony with nature
  • C. Viper – Tactical fighter with heroic inclinations; ally in strategic missions
  • Rashid – Energetic hero from Arabia; considered a friendly ally to Game Maker
  • Nash (SFV version) – Resilient hero; trusted combat ally and strategist

Street Fighter 6 Heroes

  • Lily – Young, skilled fighter; Game Maker encourages her growth and mentorship
  • Luke – Modern hero fighter; trusted in high-risk missions alongside Game Maker
  • Jamie – Energetic fighter; friendly supporter and tactical ally

Relationship Summary

Game Maker maintains strong bonds with heroes across the Street Fighter universe. The alliance focuses on teamwork, multiverse defense, and mutual respect. Characters like Ryu, Chun-Li, Ruby Heart, Cammy, Alex, and Luke serve as key allies, mentors, and friends.

Villains and corrupt characters, such as Vega and Zangief, are not trusted and excluded from this alliance due to their dangerous and unstable nature.
