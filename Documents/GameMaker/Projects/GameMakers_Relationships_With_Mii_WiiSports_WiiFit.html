Game Maker’s Relationship with Mii Channel, Wii Sports, and Wii Fit

The Mii-based universe represents the everyday heroes—ordinary people who become extraordinary through balance, discipline, and teamwork. Game Maker respects this world as a symbol of unity and self-improvement.

Mii Channel

Key Allies:

Player Miis, Plaza Miis, Default Wii Miis

Bond: Identity, Unity, and Creation

Game Maker sees the Mii Channel as the foundation of self-expression. He protects the Mii Plaza from corruption and data threats, ensuring every Mii represents individuality and free will.

Wii Sports

Key Allies:

Athlete Miis, Champions, Training Miis

Bond: Friendly Competition & Physical Mastery

Game Maker trains alongside Wii Sports Miis in Tennis, Baseball, Bowling, Golf, and Boxing. He values fair play, sportsmanship, and teamwork, helping Miis grow stronger both mentally and physically.

Wii Fit

Key Allies:

Wii Fit Trainer (Male & Female), Fitness Miis

Bond: Balance, Health, and Inner Strength

The Wii Fit Trainer guides Game Maker in maintaining balance between power and discipline. Together, they help Miis overcome fatigue, stress, and instability caused by digital threats.

Game Maker believes true strength comes from balance. Through the Mii Channel, Wii Sports, and Wii Fit, he protects the heart of everyday heroes who quietly shape the digital world.
