Game Maker & Japanese Video Game/Anime Heroes

Main Hero

Game Maker (myself, 27 years old), born March 5, 1998 in Charlotte, NC. He is the central hero who unites heroes from video games and anime to work together against antagonists like Doctor Aakom. All characters are allies, talk like humans, and coordinate in battles.

Relationships Overview

Game Maker coordinates combat, strategy, and support with all heroes above. All characters are allies, no villains or enemies included. They speak like humans and work together as a team in the digital universe.
