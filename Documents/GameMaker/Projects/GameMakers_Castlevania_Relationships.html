Alliance Status: Trusted Ally of the Belmont Bloodline

Relationship Summary

Game Maker is regarded as a **timeless ally of the Belmont legacy**—never replacing heroes, only strengthening them. His closest bonds are with Alucard, the Belmonts, and those who struggle against destiny.

Where darkness rises, Game Maker stands as a silent guardian, ensuring hope endures across centuries.
