Team Bad Guys Multiverse

This page lists the ultimate compilation of Japanese video game villains, bosses, rivals, and enemy characters across multiple franchises (1991–2025), movies, and crossover universes. Game Overload, his brothers, and Doctor Aakom coordinate schemes to destabilize heroes and conquer worlds.

Super Mario Series

  • Bowser – Super Mario; master villain, enemy of Mario & friends
  • Fawful – Mario & Luigi series; manipulative & cunning
  • Koopalings – Super Mario; Bowser’s children, loyal to schemes

Sonic Series

  • Dr. Eggman – Sonic the Hedgehog; technological mastermind
  • Mephiles – Sonic; dark entity, manipulates timelines
  • Metal Sonic – Sonic; robotic doppelganger, deadly combatant
  • Infinite – Sonic Forces; manipulates reality & fear
  • Game Overload – Doctor Aakom’s creation; allied with his brothers, manipulates machines & worlds
  • Game Overload Brothers – Doctor Aakom Multiverse; coordinated attacks, extreme chaos, rival to Game Maker

Mega Man Series

  • Dr. Wily – Mega Man; arch-nemesis & mastermind
  • Sigma – Mega Man X; resilient, cybernetic overlord

Fire Emblem Series

  • Nergal – FE: Blazing Blade; dark magic manipulator
  • Lyon – FE: Sacred Stones; corrupted by evil powers

Doctor Aakom & Game Overload

  • Doctor Aakom – Multiverse scientist villain; creates rogue AI & super-powered hybrids
  • Game Overload – Son of Doctor Aakom; chaos engine, controls machines, allied with brothers
  • Game Data, Game Creator, Game Chaos, Game Virus, Game Error, Game Glitch, Game Auto, Game Corruption, Game Pixel, Game Code, Game Relay, Game Execution, Game Crusher, Game Surge, Game Photon, Game Breaker, Game Boost, Game Burst, Game Overdrive – creations of Doctor Aakom; sealed by heroes in their respective universes; highly untrustworthy, multiversal threats
  • Evil Relationship: Doctor Aakom commands them; Game Overload coordinates them; they act as a rogue villain network across dimensions, often rebelling or corrupting worlds, rivaling Game Maker and allied heroes

Other Multiverse Villains

  • Ganondorf – The Legend of Zelda; dark power wielder
  • Sigma – Mega Man X; unstoppable cybernetic threat
  • Bowser – Mario; kingdom conqueror
  • Mephiles – Sonic; darkness manipulator

Team Bad Guys: These villains, enemies, and rivals often team up across multiverse crossovers. With Doctor Aakom at the helm, Game Overload and his brothers coordinate attacks to destabilize heroes, conquer worlds, or free their sealed siblings. Heroes like Game Maker, Doctor Fudo, and the Goodwins actively work to contain them.
