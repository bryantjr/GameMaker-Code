Game Maker’s Relationships with F-Zero Heroes

In the high-speed future of F-Zero, Game Maker allies only with honorable racers who fight for justice, protection, and peace. No rivals, no villains, no hostile characters are included.

Trusted F-Zero Allies

Captain Falcon

Legendary Racer & Justice Enforcer

Bond: Honor & Trust

Captain Falcon respects Game Maker as a fellow protector. Their partnership combines raw power, speed, and unwavering justice against galactic threats.

Dr. Stewart

Doctor & Precision Racer

Bond: Strategy & Care

Dr. Stewart provides medical expertise and calm judgment, ensuring Game Maker and allies stay battle-ready.

Jody Summer

Officer & Pilot

Bond: Cooperation & Order

Jody works alongside Game Maker to maintain order, offering intelligence and tactical coordination.

Samurai Goroh

Independent Fighter (Good Side)

Bond: Mutual Respect

Though strong-willed, Goroh recognizes Game Maker’s integrity and supports him when innocent lives are at stake.

Octoman

Mercenary Turned Ally

Bond: Redemption & Loyalty

Octoman chooses the good side under Game Maker’s guidance, standing against chaos and protecting civilians.

Support Network

F-Zero Pit Crew & Engineers

Support & Maintenance

Bond: Reliability & Teamwork

Engineers assist Game Maker with vehicle upgrades, repairs, and advanced energy systems for combat racing.

In the F-Zero universe, speed is power—but trust is everything. Game Maker and his allies race not for glory, but to protect the future.
