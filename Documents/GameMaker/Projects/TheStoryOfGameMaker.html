The Story of Game Maker

About Game Maker

Name: Game Maker

Age: 27 years old

Birthdate: March 5, 1998

Birthplace: Charlotte, NC

Origin: Inquiry to Nintendo, Sega, Capcom, Namco, Neo Geo, Game Freak, Midway, Square, Konami, Ubisoft, Atari, Rare Ware, WayForward, FUNimation, Toonami, Viz Shōnen Jump, Trigger, and other Japanese video game/animated characters.

Allies, Friends, and Supporting Heroes

Game Maker is supported by countless heroes from video games and anime across multiple series:

Video Game Allies

  • Super Mario series: Mario, Luigi, Wario (good side), Waluigi (good side), Peach, Toad, Daisy
  • Donkey Kong series: Donkey Kong, Diddy Kong, Dixie Kong, Cranky Kong
  • Legend of Zelda series: Link, Zelda, Impa
  • Metroid: Samus Aran
  • Pokemon series: Red, Leaf, Gold, Brendan, Lucas, Hilbert, Hilda, Nate, Rosa, Calem, Elio, Selene, Victor, Gloria, Florian, Juliana
  • Kirby series: Kirby allies (except non-speaking)
  • Sonic series: Sonic (good side), Tails, Knuckles, Amy, Metal Sonic allies (good side)
  • Mega Man series: Alouette, Proto Man, Bass, Treble, Tron Bonne (good side), Servbots, Morrigan (good side)
  • F-Zero, Fire Emblem, Xenoblade Chronicles, Pikmin series, Bayonetta, Castlevania, Street Fighter, Final Fantasy, Kingdom Hearts, and more

Anime Allies

  • My Hero Academia: Eri (6 years old), Midoriya (Deku), etc.
  • Boruto: Himawari (10 years old)
  • Yu-Gi-Oh series, Digimon series, One Piece, Naruto, Sailor Moon, Fairy Tail, Soul Eater, Hunter x Hunter, JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure, Bleach, and many more heroes

Special Support Characters

Small but important allies that support Game Maker in combat:

  • Alouette (Mega Man Zero)
  • Eri (My Hero Academia)
  • Himawari (Boruto Next Generations)

Major Battles

Game Maker fights against the evil Doctor Aakom (similar to Doctor Eggman/Wily) and his dangerous creations. Notable enemies include:

  • Game Overload with Neo Metal Sonic
  • Game Data from Space Colony
  • Game Chaos from Flame Core
  • Game Virus from Dark Desert
  • Game Error from Digital Dimension
  • Game Glitch from Scrap Brain Zone
  • Game Auto from Chemical Planet
  • Game Corruption from Labyrinth Zone
  • Game Pixel from Mushroom Hill
  • Game Code from Ice Cap Zone
  • Game Relay from Casino Night Zone
  • Game Execution from Rusty Ruin Zone
  • Game Crusher from Mystic Mansion
  • Game Surge from Starlight Zone
  • Game Photon from Twinkle Park

All these threats were ultimately sealed away or defeated by Game Maker and his allies.

Universe Overview

Game Maker’s universe includes heroes and good-sided characters from:

  • All major Japanese video game franchises (Nintendo, Sega, Capcom, Namco, Neo Geo, Game Freak, Square, Konami, Ubisoft, Atari, Rare Ware, WayForward)
  • Anime and cartoon heroes (FUNimation, Toonami, Viz Shōnen Jump, Trigger, Marvel, DC)
  • Video games spanning from classic (Mario, Zelda, Metroid, Pokemon) to modern (Splatoon, Xenoblade, Persona, Bayonetta, Monster Hunter)
  • Anime shows like Naruto, Boruto, My Hero Academia, One Piece, Dragon Ball, Sailor Moon, Yu-Gi-Oh, Digimon, Hunter x Hunter, Bleach, JoJo, and more

Story Goal

Game Maker’s mission is to protect the world from evil threats, seal dangerous creations, and maintain peace across all universes, aided by heroes and supporters from games, anime, and beyond.
