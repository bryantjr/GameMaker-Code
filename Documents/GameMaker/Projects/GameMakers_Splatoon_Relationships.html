Game Maker – Splatoon Multiverse Alliance

Alliance Status: Trusted Inkling & Octoling Heroes

Game Maker maintains strong, friendly, and heroic relationships with the Splatoon universe. Together, they defend their worlds from chaos, corruption, and digital threats across multiple timelines and dimensions.

Splatoon 2 (2017)

  • Agent 4 – Reliable frontline hero; Game Maker trusts them in high-risk missions
  • Callie – Cheerful supporter; emotional encouragement and morale booster
  • Marie – Strategic planner; works closely with Game Maker during operations
  • Cap’n Cuttlefish – Veteran mentor; provides wisdom and historical knowledge

Splatoon 2: Octo Expansion (2018)

  • Agent 8 – Determined survivor; strong bond with Game Maker through hardship
  • Pearl – Energetic leader; respects Game Maker’s courage and quick thinking
  • Marina – Technical genius; assists Game Maker with data, systems, and analysis
  • Iso Padre – Supportive guide; encourages growth and recovery

Splatoon 3 (2022)

  • Agent 3 (New Agent) – Core ally; fights alongside Game Maker in large-scale battles
  • Deep Cut (Shiver, Frye, Big Man) – Tactical allies; provide reconnaissance and intel
  • Return of the Squid Sisters – Continued partnership and trust

Splatoon 3: Side Order (2024)

  • Agent 8 (Return) – Stronger bond with Game Maker after shared trials
  • Marina (Central Role) – Works directly with Game Maker against digital corruption
  • Pearl (Support Role) – Emotional anchor and combat morale support
  • Order-Resistant Allies – Heroes who reject control and fight for free will

Relationship Summary

Game Maker’s relationship with the Splatoon heroes is built on **trust, resilience, and freedom**. He acts as a guardian across timelines, while Inklings and Octolings support him with creativity, strategy, and courage.

Marina and Pearl serve as his **technical and emotional support**, while the Agents stand beside him in combat as equals and friends.
