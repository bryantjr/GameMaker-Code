Game Maker – Bayonetta 1 & 2 Alliance

Alliance Status: Witch Allies & Good Side Characters

Game Maker shares strong bonds with Bayonetta and her allies, forged through battles against angels, demons, and multiversal threats. Relationships are built on mutual trust, combat synergy, and protection of the human and magical worlds.

Bayonetta (1 & 2)

  • Bayonetta – Main ally; close friendship and tactical partner in combat
  • Jeanne – Rival-turned-ally; mutual respect and trust in battles
  • Rodin – Weapon smith and strategic advisor; supports Game Maker with equipment and insight
  • Cereza (Bayonetta’s past) – Spiritual connection and mentor figure

Bayonetta 2 Allies

  • Enzo – Comic relief and occasional informant; reliable in non-combat support
  • Rosa – Magical assistant and ally; aids with arcane knowledge
  • Loki – Divine ally in key battles; trusted for strategy and intelligence
  • Kid Enemies Turned Allies – Minor angels/demons who switched allegiance to aid Bayonetta and Game Maker

Overall Relationship Summary

Game Maker’s relationship with Bayonetta and her allies is defined by **teamwork, combat synergy, and trust**. Bayonetta and Jeanne stand as his **closest combat allies**, while Rodin and Loki provide **support and strategy**. The alliance ensures that multiverse threats are met with strength, intelligence, and magical power.
