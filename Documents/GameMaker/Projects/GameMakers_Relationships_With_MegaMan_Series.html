Game Maker’s Relationships with the Mega Man Multiverse

Across time, timelines, and digital worlds, Game Maker stands alongside the heroes of the Mega Man universe. These bonds are built on justice, protection, mentorship, and hope — never villainy.

Classic Mega Man (1–8)

Original Robot Hero Era

Bond: Justice, Courage, and Legacy

  • Mega Man (Rock) – Main hero; Game Maker respects his unwavering sense of justice.
  • Roll – Support and family; emotional anchor for both Mega Man and Game Maker.
  • Proto Man – Independent protector; mutual respect and silent trust.
  • Bass & Treble – Anti-hero allies; strength-focused partnership.
  • Auto – Mechanical support and upgrades.

Mega Man X (1–8)

Reploid Era Heroes

Bond: Evolution, Responsibility, and War Prevention

  • X – Strategic ally; believes in peaceful coexistence.
  • Zero – Warrior partner; deep trust forged through battle.
  • Alia – Tactical support and intelligence.
  • Layer & Pallette – Navigation, repairs, and mission support.

Mega Man Zero (1–4)

Resistance Era

Bond: Survival, Protection of Innocents

  • Zero – Legendary hero; shared burden of leadership.
  • Ciel – Scientist and moral compass.
  • Alouette – Child support character; protected by Game Maker.
  • Leviathan, Harpuia, Fefnir, Phantom (reformed) – Honored warriors.

Mega Man ZX

Biometal Era

Bond: Identity and Choice

  • Vent – Playable hero; teamwork-focused.
  • Aile – Playable heroine; agility and leadership.
  • Prairie (excluded) – Not included per request.

Mega Man Battle Network (1–6)

Digital Net Society

Bond: Data Protection and Cyber Peace

  • Lan Hikari – Operator ally.
  • MegaMan.EXE – Digital hero; synchronization with Game Maker.
  • Mayl & Roll.EXE – Emotional and technical support.
  • Yai & Glide.EXE – Strategy and resources.

Mega Man Star Force (1–4)

EM Wave Era

Bond: Friendship Across Dimensions

  • Geo Stelar – Main hero; emotional growth.
  • Omega-Xis – Power partner.
  • Sonia Strumm – Support heroine.
  • Luna Platz – Leadership support.

Mega Man Legends

Adventure & Exploration Era

Bond: Discovery and Protection of Ruins

  • Mega Man Volnutt – Explorer hero.
  • Roll Caskett – Engineering and emotional support.
  • Barrel Caskett – Mentor figure.

The Misadventures of Tron Bonne

Reformed Allies

Bond: Redemption and Cooperation

  • Tron Bonne – Genius mechanic turned ally.
  • Servbots – Loyal helpers.
  • Tiesel Bonne – Tactical support.
The Mega Man multiverse stands as one of Game Maker’s strongest alliances — built on heroism, compassion, and the will to protect the future.
