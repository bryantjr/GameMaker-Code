Game Maker’s Relationships with Kid Icarus Heroes

Game Maker allies with the heroes of the Kid Icarus universe, including Pit, Palutena, Viridi, and other good-side characters. Together, they protect the realms, battle evil forces, and maintain peace with teamwork and courage.

Pit

Angel Warrior & Hero

Bond: Leadership & Combat Partner

Pit teams up with Game Maker in aerial battles and epic quests, lending courage and unmatched agility.

Palutena

Goddess of Light & Strategy

Bond: Guidance & Magical Support

Palutena provides strategic advice, magical powers, and intelligence to aid Game Maker in critical missions.

Viridi (Good Side)

Goddess of Nature

Bond: Nature & Tactical Support

Viridi assists Game Maker using her powers over nature and provides tactical support during challenges.

Other Angelic Allies

Himari

Young Angel Apprentice

Bond: Training & Assistance

Himari trains alongside Game Maker and aids in minor battles or reconnaissance missions.

Sky Knights

Angelic Guard Units

Bond: Protection & Combat Support

The Sky Knights provide aerial support and fight alongside Game Maker in coordinated attacks.

Magnus

Sky Guardian

Bond: Tactical Support

Magnus assists Game Maker in defense and offense strategies, especially during large-scale conflicts.

Game Maker’s alliance with Kid Icarus heroes emphasizes courage, strategy, and teamwork. Together, they protect the skies and lands, face challenges, and ensure peace in the realm of angels.
