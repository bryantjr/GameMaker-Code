The Story of Game Maker

About Game Maker

Game Maker is a 27-year-old Japanese video game character. Born on March 5, 1998, in Charlotte, North Carolina. He made inquiries and connections with legendary gaming companies and anime publishers:

  • Nintendo, Sega, Capcom, Namco, Neo Geo, Game Freak
  • Midway, Square, Konami, Ubisoft, Atari, Rare Ware, Way Forward
  • FUNimation, Toonami, Viz Shōnen Jump, Trigger
