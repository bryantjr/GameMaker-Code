Game Maker – Final Fantasy Multiverse Alliance

Alliance Status: Legendary Heroes

Game Maker maintains strong bonds with the heroes of the Final Fantasy universe. These relationships are built on trust, shared battles, destiny, and protection of worlds and crystals across timelines. Only heroic and good-aligned characters are included.

Final Fantasy I–III (Warriors of Light Era)

  • Warriors of Light – Game Maker respects them as the foundation of all hero legends
  • Firion – Trusted swordsman and freedom fighter
  • Maria – Strategic and emotional support ally
  • Onion Knight – Adaptive hero; valued for versatility

Final Fantasy IV–VI (Classic Heroic Age)

  • Cecil Harvey – Paladin ally; symbol of redemption and honor
  • Rosa – Healer and emotional anchor
  • Kain (redeemed) – Warrior ally under watchful trust
  • Bartz Klauser – Free-spirited adventurer and close friend
  • Terra Branford – Magic knight; deeply trusted by Game Maker
  • Locke Cole – Loyal protector and strategist
  • Celes Chere – Knight ally; strong bond of mutual respect

Final Fantasy VII Universe

  • Cloud Strife – Core ally; shares burden of destiny
  • Tifa Lockhart – Trusted confidant and emotional support
  • Aerith Gainsborough – Spiritual guide and light of hope
  • Barret Wallace – Comrade-in-arms and leader
  • Zack Fair – Heroic mentor spirit
  • Yuffie Kisaragi – Agile ally and scout
  • Vincent Valentine – Watchful guardian ally

Final Fantasy VIII–IX

  • Squall Leonhart – Reserved but reliable ally
  • Rinoa Heartilly – Emotional bridge between allies
  • Zidane Tribal – Optimistic and trusted partner
  • Garnet (Dagger) – Royal ally and peacekeeper
  • Vivi Ornitier – Protected friend and symbol of innocence

Final Fantasy X & X-2

  • Tidus – Energetic companion and morale booster
  • Yuna – Sacred summoner; Game Maker deeply respects her resolve

Final Fantasy XI & XIV (MMORPG Heroes)

Final Fantasy XVI

Overall Relationship Summary

Game Maker’s relationship with Final Fantasy heroes is defined by shared sacrifice, mutual respect, and protection of worlds across time and dimensions. Heroes such as Cloud, Lightning, Yuna, Noctis, Clive, and the Warriors of Light stand as his closest allies in multiversal threats.

Together, they form a united front of legends, bound by crystals, destiny, and hope.
