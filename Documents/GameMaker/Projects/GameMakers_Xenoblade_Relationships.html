Game Maker – Xenoblade Heroes Alliance

Alliance Status: Trusted Heroes

Game Maker has formed strong bonds with the heroes of the Xenoblade universe. These allies support him in multiverse events and combat scenarios. Only good-aligned characters are included; villains or morally gray characters are excluded.

Xenoblade Chronicles (XBC) Heroes

  • Shulk – Wielder of the Monado; Game Maker respects his intelligence and courage
  • Reyn – Loyal fighter and friend; Game Maker trusts him in battle and strategy
  • Fiora – Skilled swordswoman; Game Maker considers her a strong and reliable ally
  • Dunban – Experienced warrior; mentor-like figure to Game Maker in combat situations
  • Melia – Princess and powerful magic user; Game Maker values her wisdom and diplomacy
  • Riki – Cheerful and brave Nopon; Game Maker enjoys his companionship and loyalty
  • Zanza (neutralized) – Only in story, not active; Game Maker maintains awareness but doesn’t ally

Xenoblade Chronicles 2 (XBC2) Heroes

  • Rex – Blade driver; Game Maker admires his determination and leadership
  • Pyra / Mythra – Twin Blades; Game Maker trusts them as key allies and advisers
  • Nia – Brave healer and fighter; strong support for Game Maker in all missions
  • Dromarch – Nopon partner of Nia; loyal and cheerful companion
  • Zeke – Charismatic and skilled fighter; Game Maker respects his creativity and combat prowess
  • Morag – Tactical and disciplined; Game Maker relies on her strategy and guidance
  • Brighid – Blade partner of Zeke; courageous and reliable ally
  • Akhos – Skilled swordsman; Game Maker values his support in multiverse crises
  • Perun – Blade partner of Akhos; trusted fighter alongside Game Maker
  • Pyra/Mythra (dual persona) – Central to alliance; Game Maker maintains strong personal bond with both forms
  • Other Blades (good-aligned only) – Lora, Electra, Agate, KOS-MOS (if crossovers), etc.; all serve as allies and mentors

Relationship Summary

Game Maker’s relationships with Xenoblade heroes are based on trust, teamwork, and multiverse defense. Characters like Shulk, Rex, Pyra/Mythra, Nia, Dunban, and Melia act as key allies, advisors, and companions in both combat and story-driven events.

Villains, antagonistic forces, or morally ambiguous figures are excluded to maintain a strong heroic alliance. All heroes support Game Maker as family, friends, and battle companions.
