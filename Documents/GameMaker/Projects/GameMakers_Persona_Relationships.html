Game Maker – Persona Multiverse Bonds

Multiverse Role:

Game Maker exists as a rare wildcard entity—recognized by Philemon’s influence— who supports humanity’s fight against Shadows, fate, and distorted desires without overriding free will.

Overall Relationship Summary

Game Maker is known across the Persona multiverse as a **guardian of free will**— someone who helps heroes confront Shadows, truth, and despair without deciding their choices for them.

His strongest bonds are with wildcard users: Tatsuya, Makoto, Yu, and Ren. Among attendants, he is seen as a rare constant that exists outside normal contracts.
