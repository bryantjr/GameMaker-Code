Game Maker’s Relationships with Fire Emblem Heroes

Game Maker allies only with the heroic champions of Fire Emblem across multiple timelines and continents. These bonds are built on courage, leadership, and protecting innocent lives. Heroes from the following games are included:
  • Fire Emblem Original
  • Fire Emblem: The Blazing Blade
  • Fire Emblem: The Sacred Stones
  • Fire Emblem: Path of Radiance
  • Fire Emblem: Radiant Dawn
  • Fire Emblem: Awakening
  • Fire Emblem: Fates
  • Fire Emblem: Three Houses

Legendary Lords

Marth

Hero-King of Archanea

Bond: Honor & Leadership

Marth and Game Maker share mutual respect. Together, they inspire armies and lead with unwavering courage.

Alm

Bond: Determination & Justice

Alm works with Game Maker to protect his kingdom and lead his allies with strong convictions.

Luthier & Eirika

Game Maker supports their mission to restore peace in Renais, guiding them with strategy and foresight.

Ike

Mercenary Hero of Crimea & Daein

Bond: Strength & Justice

Ike trusts Game Maker as a reliable ally, fighting alongside him to uphold justice and protect lives.

Roy

Bond: Growth & Responsibility

Game Maker mentors Roy, helping him become a wise and compassionate leader.

Micaiah

Micaiah values Game Maker’s support in strategy and moral guidance during her trials.

United by light, courage, and purpose, Game Maker and the Fire Emblem heroes protect worlds from collapse—without rivalry, hatred, or darkness.
