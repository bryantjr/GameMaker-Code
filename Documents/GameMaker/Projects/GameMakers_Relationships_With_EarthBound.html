Game Maker’s Relationships with EarthBound

In the strange yet heartfelt world of EarthBound, Game Maker allies with heroes who fight using courage, kindness, and PSI powers. Only good-side characters are included.

Main Heroes

Ness

PSI Prodigy

Bond: Courage & Inner Strength

Ness senses Game Maker’s heroic heart. Together, they face cosmic threats with calm determination and powerful PSI abilities.

Paula

PSI Support & Hope Bearer

Bond: Faith & Emotional Support

Paula’s prayers strengthen Game Maker during the darkest battles, restoring hope when odds seem impossible.

Jeff

Genius Inventor

Bond: Technology & Strategy

Jeff collaborates with Game Maker to create gadgets, defensive tools, and advanced battle support systems.

Poo

Martial Artist & Mystic

Bond: Discipline & Wisdom

Poo trains alongside Game Maker, teaching balance between power and restraint.

Allies & Supporters

King

Loyal Guardian

Bond: Protection & Loyalty

King instinctively trusts Game Maker and stays close to protect the team from unseen danger.

Apple Kid

Inventor Ally

Bond: Innovation & Curiosity

Apple Kid shares inventions and experimental ideas to help Game Maker overcome strange obstacles.

Orange Kid

Creative Thinker

Bond: Competition & Growth

Orange Kid challenges Game Maker’s creativity, pushing new solutions to unusual problems.

Mr. Saturn

Peaceful Helper

Bond: Healing & Friendship

The Mr. Saturns welcome Game Maker warmly, offering healing, rest, and emotional support.

Spiritual Connections

Buzz Buzz

Time Messenger

Bond: Destiny & Warning

Buzz Buzz entrusts Game Maker with knowledge of future threats and the responsibility to protect Earth.

With kindness as their greatest weapon, Game Maker and the EarthBound heroes protect the world from cosmic danger while preserving innocence and hope.
