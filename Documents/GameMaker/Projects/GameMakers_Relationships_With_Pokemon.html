Game Maker’s Relationships with Pokémon Heroes

Game Maker allies with Pokémon Trainers across generations. Together, they protect Pokémon, preserve balance, and stop threats without domination or corruption. All characters listed are heroes.

Bond: Partnership & Exploration

Pokémon Let’s Go Pikachu / Eevee
